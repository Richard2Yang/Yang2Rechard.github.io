A Guideline for Successful Calibration and Uncertainty Analysis for Soil and Water Assessment: A Review of Papers from the 2016 International SWAT Conference

2019-11-04 SWAT Model Literature Calibration SWAT-CUP SCI Review Validation

Ciation:

Abbaspour, Karim & Ashraf Vaghefi, Saeid & Srinivasan, Raghvan. (2017). A Guideline for Successful Calibration and Uncertainty Analysis for Soil and Water Assessment: A Review of Papers from the 2016 International SWAT Conference. Water. 10. 6. 10.3390/w10010006.

Keywords:

calibration; validation; uncertainty analysis; sensitivity analysis; pre-calibration analysis; SWAT-CUP

Key points:

  • Review on calibration and uncertainty analysis of SWAT Model.
    对于SWAT模型在参数率定和验证过程的综述
  • Highlight a good protocal for calibrating a SWAT model with SWAT-CUP.
    指出了使用SWAT-CUP率定SWAT模型参数的一些规范

What I've learned：

  • Steps for builing hydrologic model

    • Creating the model with a hydrologic program/运行模型
    • Performing sensitivity analysis/敏感性分析(确定模型中最关键的影响因素)
    • Performing calibration and uncertainty analysis/不确定性分析与参数率定(比较模拟值与实测值，确定模拟的精度)
    • Validating the model/模型验证(确定参数率定的可信度)
    • Performing risk analysis(optional)/危险性分析(少数情况下)

  • Protocals for Calibration for SWAT

    • Pre-calibration input data and model structure improvemnet/选最优的输入数据
    • Identify the parameters to optimize/确定需要优化的参数
    • Identify other sensitive parameters/确定其他敏感性参数
    • Running the model/运行模型
    • Perform post-processing/后处理
    • Modifying the suggested new parameters/修改超出所需范围或物理意义范围的参数
