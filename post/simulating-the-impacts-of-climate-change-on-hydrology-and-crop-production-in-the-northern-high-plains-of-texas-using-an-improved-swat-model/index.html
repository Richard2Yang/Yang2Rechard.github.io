Simulating the impacts of climate change on hydrology and crop production in the Northern High Plains of Texas using an improved SWAT model

2019-11-05 SWAT Model Literature Hydrological Model SCI

Ciation:

Chen, Y., G. W. Marek, T. H. Marek, et al.:Simulating the impacts of climate change on hydrology and crop production in the Northern High Plains of Texas using an improved SWAT model, Agricultural Water Management 221: 13-24,doi:10.1016/j.agwat.2019.04.021,2019.

Keywords:

Dryland; Fallow; General circulation model (GCM); Irrigation; Evapotranspiration; Yield

Key points:

  • An overview of the impact of climate change on the water use and crop production under different land uses in the NHP to sustain the identification and development of climate adaptation strategies at local to regional scales/概述了德克萨斯北部高平原地区(NHPT,Northern High Plains of Texas)不同土地用途下气候变化对水资源利用和作物生产的影响，以维持在地方到区域范围内气候适应战略的确定和发展

What I've Learned:

  • Main Conclusions
    • The elevated CO2CO_2CO2​ level of future climate scenarios was the primary factor for the decrease in the ET and irrigation/未来气候情景中较高的CO2CO_2CO2​水平是ET和灌溉减少的主要因素
    • The reduction in future crop yield was mainly attributed to the shortening of the maturity period caused by increased future temperature/未来作物产量下降的主要原因是由于未来温度升高而导致的成熟期缩短
  • Useful Tips
    • The future climate data projected that temperatures will increase significantly, particularly during the crop growing season (May to October)， while the future annual precipitation will decrease by 3%–8% and mainly in months of January and February/预测数据显示未来(21世纪末之前)夏秋季节(5-10月)气温将显著增加，冬季(1,2月)降水则明显降低
  • Unfamiliar Worlds
    • crop yield 作物产量
    • elevated 提高的，高架
    • irrigation 灌溉
    • ET(Evapotranspiration) 蒸散发
    • stomatal 气孔
